INDIAN TOURISM

Iconic sites like the Taj Mahal, Jaipur's forts, and Varanasi's ghats showcase its architectural brilliance and spiritual significance. Wildlife sanctuaries like Ranthambore and Kaziranga attract nature enthusiasts, while festivals like Diwali and Holi add color to the experience.
