HIMACHAL PRADESH

1. Manali

Manali, a popular hill station, is known for its breathtaking views of the Himalayas. It offers adventure sports like skiing, paragliding, and trekking. The nearby Rohtang Pass and Solang Valley are perfect for snow sports. The spiritual Hidimba Devi Temple and Vashisht Hot Springs add cultural charm.

2. Shimla

Shimla, the summer capital during British rule, is famous for its colonial architecture, pleasant weather, and scenic landscapes. The Ridge, Mall Road, and Jakhoo Temple are major attractions. Visitors can also enjoy the toy train ride from Kalka to Shimla, offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills.
