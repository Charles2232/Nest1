RAJASTHAN

1. Jaipur (The Pink City)

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known for its rich history and architectural marvels like the Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal. The vibrant bazaars offer colorful textiles and jewelry, while the Jantar Mantar observatory showcases ancient astronomical instruments, making it a cultural and historical hub.

2. Udaipur

Udaipur, often called the "City of Lakes," is renowned for its picturesque lakes, including Lake Pichola. The City Palace, with its stunning architecture and panoramic views, stands as a symbol of royalty. Boat rides on the lake, visiting Jag Mandir and the serene ambiance make Udaipur a romantic destination.

3. Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer, the "Golden City," is famous for its stunning sandstone architecture, including the Jaisalmer Fort, which rises majestically from the desert.
