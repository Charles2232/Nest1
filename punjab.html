PUNJAB

1. Golden Temple (Amritsar)

The Golden Temple, or Harmandir Sahib, is a sacred Sikh pilgrimage site renowned for its divine beauty and tranquility. Its golden dome and surrounding reflective pool create a serene atmosphere. Visitors can experience the free community kitchen (Langar) and the spiritual ambiance that attracts millions each year.

2. Wagah Border (Attari)

The Wagah Border, between India and Pakistan, is famous for the daily flag-lowering ceremony. The spirited display of patriotism, with soldiers performing synchronized drills, is an emotional experience for visitors. The ceremony, held at dusk, draws large crowds and offers a unique glimpse into the tension and cooperation between the two nations.

3. Jallianwala Bagh (Amritsar)

Jallianwala Bagh is a historic park that commemorates the tragic 1919 massacre where British troops opened fire on peaceful Indian protesters.
